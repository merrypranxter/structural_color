# Resources

## Books

### Physics and Optics
- **Hecht, E. (2017).** *Optics* (5th ed.). Pearson.
  - The standard undergraduate optics textbook
  - Chapters 9-10 cover interference and diffraction

- **Born, M. & Wolf, E. (1999).** *Principles of Optics* (7th ed.). Cambridge.
  - The definitive reference for classical optics
  - Comprehensive coverage of electromagnetic theory

- **Macleod, H.A. (2017).** *Thin-Film Optical Filters* (5th ed.). CRC Press.
  - Specialized treatment of thin-film interference
  - Includes design techniques

### Structural Color and Biology
- **Kinoshita, S. (2008).** *Structural Colors in the Realm of Nature*. World Scientific.
  - Comprehensive overview of biological structural color
  - Beautiful photography and diagrams

- **Parker, A.R. (2006).** "Seven deadly colours." *BioEssays* 28, 1.
  - Review article on structural color in nature

- **Vukusic, P. & Sambles, J.R. (2003).** "Photonic structures in biology." *Nature* 424, 852.
  - Seminal paper on biological photonic crystals

### Photonic Crystals
- **Joannopoulos, J.D. et al. (2008).** *Photonic Crystals* (2nd ed.). Princeton.
  - The standard reference for photonic crystal physics
  - Available free online

- **Busch, K. et al. (2007).** "Photonic crystals." *Physics Reports* 444, 101.
  - Comprehensive review article
